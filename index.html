Frequently Asked Questions

  • what kind of payment options do you offer?
    • We are happy to split your project total into two, three, our four installments depending on what works best for you. For those who prefer to pay in full upon booking, we also offer a 5% discount!
  • i'm not very tech-savvy. can you help us learn to use our new website?
    • We primarily specialize in WordPress & Shopify websites, though also work on ShowIt or Squarespace. Not sure what's best for your brand? We are happy to help recommend the one that would suit your specific needs best.
  • my content won't be ready for a while. can we still work together?
    • Absolutely!! We aim to create websites that are user-friendly for both you AND your customer. At the end of each website project, we record a screenshare video tutorial detailing the ins & outs of your new site so you can learn to use it moving forward. We also offer 30 days of post-launch support as a part of all website projects so that we've got your back in the event questions arise after your site goes live.
  • do you offer additional brand services such as seo or marketing help?
    • We typically find it's best to have your content ready to go by your start date as it allows the project to flow more effectively. However, we understand this may not always be possible & are happy to discuss what options might work best for your situation including booking a later start date, sourcing stock photographer, & referring copywriters.
  • do you offer retainer services?
    • Yes! We are beginning to introduce these into our offerings & would love to talk with you to see how we may be able to help. Please contact us for more information!
